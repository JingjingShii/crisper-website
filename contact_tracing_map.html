Contact Tracing Map

This Web App gathers Exposure Site information from multiple State Government sites and presents the locations on a map.
Automated Alerts

Automated Alerts supports daily COVID-19 situation updates for your specific locations of interest. Reports are sent by email. Public data from across Australia's various Departments of Health are utilised.
Other Dashboards

Find other useful dashboards visualising COVID-19 Data, from National Summary Graphs to Cases by day released by governments.
Data Engine

The CRISPER Data Engine is a resource supporting the Automatic Alerts and the Dashboards. The Data Engine provides access to many data sources collected into one place..
About

Our focus is on the delivery of notifications of situation updates to users, who may include the general public, GP practices, epidemiologists, and other health professionals. Daily emailed automated alerts can be managed here and mobile notifications will be supported through our native smart phone App.
