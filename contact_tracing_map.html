Contact Tracing Alert Locations Map



The map shows all current contact tracing alert locations (locations where potential exposure to infectious COVID-19 cases may have occurred). Zoom in to your area of interest. The icons can be tapped to show the actual details. Icons include specific locations (red points) and transportation (trains, buses, flights, ferries, etc). Alerts can be filtered by date, state, and postcode. Search functions by postcode, suburb or address also allow alert locations to be easily identified. Tap the crosshair icon to zoom into your current location.

The Web App was developed with mobile phone capability so users can easily check for new exposure locations and visualise areas with multiple alerts. The Web App for Smart Phones can be installed as an App on your smart phone by loading the URL mobile.crisper.net.au into your browser and then choose to Add to Home Screen (from the browser menu). This will install an icon on your home screen to directly access the App.

The Contact Tracing Alert Locations Map was developed by the ANU's Research School of Population Health's GRAPHC team: Michael Hewett and Paul Konings. The underlying data is sourced from CRISPER's Data Engine.
