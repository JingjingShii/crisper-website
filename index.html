Contact Tracing Map

The Web App gathers Exposure Site information from multiple State Government sites and presents the locations on a map. Check for locations near you and your loved ones.
Automated Alerts

Automated Alerts supports daily COVID-19 situation updates for your specific locations of interest. Reports are sent by email or displayed online. Public data from across Australia's various Departments of Health are utilised.
Other Dashboards

Other useful dashboards developed by the ANU's GRAPHC team for visualising COVID-19 Data, from National Summary Graphs to Cases by Day released by governments.
Data Engine

The CRISPER Data Engine is a resource supporting the Automatic Alerts and the Dashboards. The Data Engine provides access to many cleansed data sources collected into one place.
